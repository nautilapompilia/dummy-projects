About Me

Our Services

Classic Tint

A classic tint is a semi-permanent tint that can last up to 5 days on the skin and 3-4 weeks on the brow hairs. This is ideal for first timers looking to achieve a “filled in” Brow. The perfect way to enhance your brow shape by adding a natural tint, creating a soft natural finish.

Brow Lamination

Brow Lamination is A 3 step brow perm that perms your brow hairs. Brow Lamination can last up to 6-8 weeks. Brow Lamination can help create a sleek, groomed look, giving the illusion of a thicker and more well-defined eyebrows. If you want “sleek”, “Fluffy” or have your curly brows tamed! This might be your perfect service.

Hybrid Stain

Hybrid Stain is a long lasting tint that stains the skin and the brow hairs. This can last up to 7-10 days on the skin and 6 weeks on the brow hairs. If you are wanting to save time in the morning, having a more defined brow look or struggle with sparse brow hairs, This service is perfect for you.

Lamination+Hybrid Stain+Wax

Elevate your brow game with our exclusive Brow Lamination + Hybrid Stain and Waxing service, designed to sculpt, tint, and define your brows to perfection for a stunning, long-lasting look that complements your unique style.

Eyebrow Wax

Discover the art of brow shaping with our precision waxing service, sculpting your brows to perfection for a defined and flawless look that enhances your natural beauty.
